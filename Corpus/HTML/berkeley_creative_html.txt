			<h2 class="inner-title">Courses in Creative Writing</h2>     			
			  <div id="content" >
			  
			    <div class="region region-content">
    <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system"><div class="widget bottom"><div id="node-3477" class="node node-full-width clearfix">

      
  
  <div class="content clearfix">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h3>Courses That Satisfy the Creative Writing Requirements</h3>
<div>Five approved upper-division courses are required, consisting of three courses in creative writing and two courses in literature. These courses may be taken in any of the departments that offer them. See <a href="requirements">Requirements</a>.</div>
<p>Courses in both creative writing and literature are sometimes listed in the <a href="http://guide.berkeley.edu/">Berkeley Academic Guide</a> under very general headings ("Topics in ________" or "Special Topics"). Students may be allowed to include courses that are not on the following list with approval of the Creative Writing Minor Faculty Adviser. It is the responsibility of the student to provide the Faculty Adviser with documentary evidence to support the claim of course eligibility. Contact the Creative Writing Minor Student Academic Advisor at <a href="mailto:demir@berkeley.edu">demir@berkeley.edu</a> for more information.</p>
<p>To determine if a course from the list below is being offered during a specific semester, visit the <a class="course-link" href="http://schedule.berkeley.edu/">Schedule of Classes</a>.</p>
<p>NOTE: <strong>Three</strong> courses are required from this list. <strong>All</strong> of these courses must be taken for a letter grade.</p>

<p class="p2"><strong><a href="http://as.ugis.berkeley.edu/">American Studies</a><br /></strong>C174. Visual Autobiography</p>

Seminar-Workshop in Creative Writing<br />141. Seminar-Workshop on Creative Writing: The Novel</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><a href="http://creative.ugis.berkeley.edu/">Creative Writing</a><br /></strong>120. Reading and Writing Poetry<br />130. Creative Prose</p>
<p class="p1"><strong><a href="http://english.berkeley.edu/">English</a><br /></strong>141. Modes of Writing (Exposition, Fiction, Verse, Etc.)<br />N141. <p class="p3"><strong>Visual Studies<br /></strong>C185A. Visual Autobiography</p>

</tr></tbody></table><p>The Creative Writing Minor is offered by the Office of Undergraduate and Interdisciplinary Studies in the Undergraduate Division of the College of Letters and Science. The courses students take to satisfy the minor course requirements are offered by various departments around campus. If you have questions regarding the minor program and whether a course not on this list may be used for the minor, contact the Creative Writing Minor Student Academic Advisor at <a href="mailto:demir@berkeley.edu">demir@berkeley.edu</a>. If you have questions regarding an approved course on this list, contact the department and instructor offering the course. The departments and instructors determine when their courses will be offered and who is eligible to take their courses.      </p>

    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><h3>Courses That Satisfy the Creative Writing Requirements</h3>
<p>Five approved upper-division courses are required, consisting of three courses in creative writing and two courses in literature. These courses may be taken in any of the departments that offer them. See <a href="requirements">Requirements</a>.</p>
<p>Courses in both creative writing and literature are sometimes listed in the <a href="http://guide.berkeley.edu/">Berkeley Academic Guide</a> under very general headings ("Topics in ________" or "Special Topics"). Students may be allowed to include courses that are not on the following list with approval of the Creative Writing Minor Faculty Adviser. It is the responsibility of the student to provide the Faculty Adviser with documentary evidence to support the claim of course eligibility. Contact the Creative Writing Student Academic Advisor at <a href="mailto:demir@berkeley.edu">demir@berkeley.edu</a> for more information.</p>
<p>To determine if a course from the list below is being offered during a specific semester, visit the <a class="course-link" href="http://schedule.berkeley.edu/">Schedule of Classes</a>.</p>
<p>NOTE: <strong>Two</strong> courses are required from this list. <strong>Both</strong> of these courses must be taken for a letter grade.           </p>

</tr></tbody></table><p>The Creative Writing Minor is offered by the Office of Undergraduate and Interdisciplinary Studies in the Undergraduate Division of the College of Letters and Science. The courses students take to satisfy the minor course requirements are offered by various departments around campus. If you have questions regarding the minor program and whether a course not on this list may be used for the minor, contact the Creative Writing Minor Student Academic Advisor at <a href="mailto:demir@berkeley.edu">demir@berkeley.edu</a>. If you have questions regarding an approved course on this list, contact the department and instructor offering the course. The departments and instructors determine when their courses will be offered and who is eligible to take their courses. </p>

    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>Five approved upper-division courses are required, consisting of three courses in creative writing and two courses in literature. These courses may be taken in any of the departments that offer them. See <a href="requirements">Requirements</a>.</p>
<p>Courses in both creative writing and literature are sometimes listed in the <a href="http://guide.berkeley.edu/">Berkeley Academic Guide</a> under very general headings ("Topics in ________" or "Special Topics"). Students may be allowed to include courses that are not on the following list with approval of the Creative Writing Minor Faculty Adviser. It is the responsibility of the student to provide the Faculty Adviser with documentary evidence to support the claim of course eligibility. Contact the Creative Writing Minor Student Academic Advisor at <a href="mailto:demir@berkeley.edu">demir@berkeley.edu</a> for more information.</p>
<p>To determine if a course from the list below is being offered during a specific semester, visit the <a class="course-link" href="http://schedule.berkeley.edu/">Schedule of Classes</a>.</p>
<p>NOTE: <strong>Two</strong> courses are required from this list. <strong>Both</strong> of these courses must be taken for a letter grade.        </p>

			<h2 class="inner-title">Graduate Programs</h2>     			
			  <div id="content" >
			  
			    <div class="region region-content">
    <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system"><div class="widget bottom"><div id="node-3481" class="node node-full-width clearfix">

      
  
  <div class="content clearfix">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>University of California, Berkeley Graduate Admissions Office</p>
<p><a href="http://www.grad.berkeley.edu/prospective/">http://www.grad.berkeley.edu/prospective/</a></p>
<p> </p>
<p>The University of California, Berkeley does not offer a graduate program in Creative Writing. Below is a list of selected Creative Writing graduate programs offered by other colleges and universities:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.antiochla.edu/index.php">ANTIOCH UNIVERSITY</a> - Los Angeles, California <a href="http://www.antiochla.edu/academics/mfa-creative-writing/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.asu.edu/asuweb/">ARIZONA STATE</a> - Tempe, Arizona<a href="http://www.asu.edu/clas/english/creativewriting/"> Creative Writing M.F.A</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.bu.edu/">BOSTON UNIVERSITY</a> - Boston, Massachusetts <a href="http://www.bu.edu/writing/">Creative Writing M.F.A</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.brooklyn.cuny.edu/">BROOKLYN COLLEGE</a> - Brooklyn, New York <a href="http://depthome.brooklyn.cuny.edu/english/graduate/mfa/geninfo.htm">Creative Writing M.F.A</a>.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.columbia.edu/">COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY</a> - New York, New York <a href="http://www.columbia.edu/cu/arts/writing/">Creative Writing M.F.A</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.concordia.ca/">CONCORDIA UNIVERSITY</a> - Montreal, Quebec, Canada <a href="http://english.concordia.ca/graduate/programs/maprograms/">M.A. in English (Creative Writing Option)</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.gmu.edu/">GEORGE MASON UNIVERSITY</a> - Fairfax, Virginia <a href="http://creativewriting.gmu.edu/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.goddard.edu/">GODDARD COLLEGE</a> - Plainfield, Vermont <a href="http://www.goddard.edu/masterfinearts_writing">Creative Writing M.F.A</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.indiana.edu/">INDIANA UNIVERSITY</a> - Bloomington, Indiana <a href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7emfawrite/">Creative Writing M.F.A. and M.A. in Fiction and Poetry</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.nyu.edu/">NEW YORK UNIVERSITY </a>- New York, New York <a href="http://www.nyu.edu/gsas/program/cwp/">Creative Writing M.F.A. or M.A. in English with Concentration in Creative Writing</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.stmarys-ca.edu/">SAINT MARY'S COLLEGE OF CALIFORNIA</a> - Moraga, California <a href="http://www.stmarys-ca.edu/mfa-in-creative-writing">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.sfsu.edu/">SAN FRANCISCO STATE UNIVERSITY</a> - San Francisco, California <a href="http://www.sfsu.edu/%7ecwriting/">Creative Writing M.A. and M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.txstate.edu/">TEXAS STATE UNIVERSITY</a> - San Marcos, <a href="http://www.english.txstate.edu/mfa/">Texas Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.stanford.edu/">STANFORD UNIVERSITY</a> <a href="http://creativewriting.stanford.edu/">Stegner Fellowship</a> - Palo Alto, California</p>
<p><a href="http://www.uab.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF ALABAMA</a> - Birmingham, Alabama M.A.in <a href="http://www.uab.edu/cas/english/academic-programs/creative-writing/graduate-concentration-in-creative-writing">English with a Concentration in Creative Writing</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.arizona.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF ARIZONA</a> - Tucson, <a href="https://grad.arizona.edu/programs/programinfo/CRTVMFA">Arizona Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uark.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF ARKANSAS</a> - Fayetteville, Arkansas <a href="http://mfa.uark.edu/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.ubc.ca/">UNIVERSITY OF BRITISH COLUMBIA</a> - Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada <a href="http://www.creativewriting.ubc.ca/programs_mfa_about.shtml">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a> and <a href="http://creativewriting.ubc.ca/prospective-students/graduate/joint-mfa-in-screenwriting/">MFA in Film Production and Creative Writing (Screenwriting)</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.ucdavis.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, DAVIS</a> - Davis, California <a href="http://english.ucdavis.edu/graduate/masters-creative-writing">English M.A. with an Emphasis in Creative Writing</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uci.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, IRVINE</a> - Irvine, California <a href="http://www.humanities.uci.edu/english/programs/mfa.php">English M.F.A. (Writing Program) </a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.ucr.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, RIVERSIDE</a> - Riverside, California <a href="http://creativewriting.ucr.edu/">M.F.A. in Creative Writing &amp; Writing for the Performing Arts</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uea.ac.uk/">UNIVERSITY OF EAST ANGLIA</a> - Norwich, United Kingdom <a href="http://www.uea.ac.uk/creativewriting">Creative Writing M.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.ufl.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF FLORIDA</a> - Gainesville, Florida <a href="http://web.english.ufl.edu/crw/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uh.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF HOUSTON</a> - Houston, Texas <a href="http://www.uh.edu/cwp/">M.A. and Ph.D. in Literature and Creative Writing and M.F.A. in English: Creative Writing</a></p>
<p><a href="https://www.uidaho.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO</a> - Moscow, <a href="https://www.uidaho.edu/class/english/graduate/mfa-creative-writing">Idaho Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uiowa.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF IOWA</a> - Iowa City, Iowa <a href="http://grad.admissions.uiowa.edu/academics/creative-writing-mfa-english">Creative Writing M.F.A. in English</a> and <a href="http://english.uiowa.edu/graduate-program/mfa-nonfiction-writing">M.F.A. in Nonfiction Writing</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.umd.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF MARYLAND</a> - College Park, Maryland <a href="http://www.english.umd.edu/academics/creativewriting/mfa">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.umich.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN</a> - Ann Arbor, Michigan <a href="http://www.lsa.umich.edu/english/grad/mfa/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.umt.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF MONTANA</a> - Missoula, <a href="http://www.cas.umt.edu/creativewriting/graduate/default.php">Montana Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.unlv.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF NEVADA</a> - Las Vegas, Nevada <a href="http://english.unlv.edu/mfa_program.html">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uoregon.edu/">UNIVERSITY OF OREGON</a> - Eugene, Oregon <a href="http://darkwing.uoregon.edu/%7ecrwrweb/">Creative Writing M.F.A.</a></p>
