            <p><strong><strong>Opening Times</strong>:</strong></p>
<p>Term-Time Hours - 9.30am to 5.30pm</p>
<p>Vacation Hours (19.06.17 - 03.10.17) - 9.15am to 4.30pm</p>
<p><strong>Admission Policy</strong></p>
<p>The Library admits members of the Faculty and others by arrangement. Borrowing is restricted to members of the Faculty. Bags and coats may not be brought into the Library. Lockers are available.</p>
<p><strong>Lending Policy</strong></p>
<p>Undergraduates may borrow 5 books for one week during term time. Postgraduates may borrow 10 books for one month.</p>
<p>Fines for normal loans will be charged at 25p per item per day.</p>
<p>Fines for overnight loans will be charged at 25p per item per hour.</p>
<p>Please make sure that your borrowing record is kept up-to-date.</p>
