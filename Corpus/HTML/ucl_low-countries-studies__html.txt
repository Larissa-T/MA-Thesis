	<h2 class="heading">Seminars</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries History Seminar 2016/17</h3>





<p class="p"><strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL), Joanna
Woodall (Courtauld)</p>
<p class="p">Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as
well as from Britain, the Low Countries seminar is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep
up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>


<h4 class="heading">2016/2017 Seminar Series </h4>




<p class="p">Meetings: Fridays at 5:15 pm at the <a href="http://www.history.ac.uk/events/seminars/131" target="_self">Institute for Historical
Research</a>. Except where indicated otherwise, all meetings take place in the <strong>Wolfson Room NB01</strong>, Basement, IHR, North block, Senate House<strong>, </strong>WC1E 7HU.</p>

	<h2 class="heading">2013/14 Archive</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"> This annual series sees regular seminars held about ten times over the course of each academic
session. Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as
well as from Britain, it is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep
up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>
<p class="p"> Multidisciplinary, it includes talks on art history and literary history, as well
as history narrowly defined.</p>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"> <strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL)<br /></p>

up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>

	<h2 class="heading">2015/16 Archive</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"><strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL), Joanna Woodall (Courtauld)</p>
<p class="p">Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as well as from Britain, the Low Countries seminar is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>


<h4 class="heading">2015/2016 Seminar Series </h4>




<p class="p">Meetings: Fridays at 5:15 pm at the <a href="http://www.history.ac.uk/events/seminars/131" target="_self">Institute for Historical Research</a>. Except where indicated otherwise, all meetings take place in the <strong>Wolfson Room NB01</strong>, Basement, IHR, North block, Senate House<strong>, </strong>WC1E 7HU.</p>

	<h2 class="heading">Seminars</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries History Seminar 2016/17</h3>





<p class="p"><strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL), Joanna
Woodall (Courtauld)</p>
<p class="p">Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as
well as from Britain, the Low Countries seminar is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep
up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>


<h4 class="heading">2016/2017 Seminar Series </h4>




<p class="p">Meetings: Fridays at 5:15 pm at the <a href="http://www.history.ac.uk/events/seminars/131" target="_self">Institute for Historical
Research</a>. Except where indicated otherwise, all meetings take place in the <strong>Wolfson Room NB01</strong>, Basement, IHR, North block, Senate House<strong>, </strong>WC1E 7HU.</p>

	<h2 class="heading">2013/14 Archive</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"> This annual series sees regular seminars held about ten times over the course of each academic
session. Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as
well as from Britain, it is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep
up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>
<p class="p"> Multidisciplinary, it includes talks on art history and literary history, as well
as history narrowly defined.</p>

	<h2 class="heading">2014/15 Archive</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"> <strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL)<br /></p>
<p class="p"> Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as
well as from Britain, the Low Countries seminar is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep
up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>


<h4 class="heading">2014/2015 Seminar Series </h4>




<p class="p"> Friday afternoons at 5:15pm.</p>
<p class="p"> <strong>Venue: Past and Present Room 202, 2nd floor, Institute of Historical Research, Senate House.</strong></p>

	<h2 class="heading">2015/16 Archive</h2>

<h3 class="heading">Low Countries Seminar</h3>





<p class="p"><strong>Convenors: </strong>Anne Goldgar (KCL), Benjamin Kaplan (UCL), Ulrich Tiedau (UCL), Joanna Woodall (Courtauld)</p>
<p class="p">Featuring speakers from the Low Countries and further afield, as well as from Britain, the Low Countries seminar is an opportunity for scholars and students to keep up-to-date on the latest work in Low Countries history.</p>


<h4 class="heading">2015/2016 Seminar Series </h4>




<p class="p">Meetings: Fridays at 5:15 pm at the <a href="http://www.history.ac.uk/events/seminars/131" target="_self">Institute for Historical Research</a>. Except where indicated otherwise, all meetings take place in the <strong>Wolfson Room NB01</strong>, Basement, IHR, North block, Senate House<strong>, </strong>WC1E 7HU.</p>
